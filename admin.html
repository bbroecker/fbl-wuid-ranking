📋 Scoring Type Descriptions

  • For Time: Athletes complete a set amount of work. Faster time = better rank. If not finished in time cap, check the "Did not finish" box and enter reps completed. Athletes with DNF rank below those who finished. (e.g., "21-15-9 Thrusters & Pull-ups")
  • AMRAP - Reps: Athletes complete as many reps as possible in time cap. More reps = better rank. (e.g., "20 min AMRAP")
  • Max Weight: Athletes lift maximum weight. Higher weight = better rank. (e.g., "1RM Snatch" or "Total of Clean & Jerk")
  • Total Reps: Similar to AMRAP but might have specific structure. More reps = better rank.

⚖️ Tiebreaker Thresholds (For Time Workouts)

For "For Time" workouts where athletes don't finish, you can add tiebreaker thresholds to rank athletes with the same rep count.

How it works:
• Enter rep counts (comma-separated) where athletes record their time (e.g., "15,63,107")
• Athletes with same reps are ranked by their time at these checkpoints
• Athletes who provide tiebreaker times rank higher than those who don't
• Example: If two athletes both have 110 reps, compare their time at 107 reps

Perfect for workouts inspired by the CrossFit Open where tiebreakers are at specific rep counts!

👁️ Workout Visibility

Use the "Visible to Athletes" checkbox to control when workouts appear on the main page. This is perfect for announcing workouts one at a time (like the CrossFit Open). Hidden workouts won't appear in the input forms or rankings, and won't count toward overall standings until you make them visible.
